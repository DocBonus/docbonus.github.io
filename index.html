Universal Algebra · Independent Research

Identities of the Graph Variety Axiomatized by x²y ≈ xy²

I attempted to characterise the graph variety Mod𝒢{x²y ≈ xy²} which is the class of all directed graphs whose graph algebras satisfy the term equation x²y ≈ xy². I found a structural theorem characterising membership via loop-uniformity within connected components, and an identity theorem giving necessary and sufficient conditions for a term equation to be an identity of the variety.

Joint work with Tiang Poomsa-ard. Presented March 2024.
