Unlocking the univerese: Redisvovering the Higgs Boson.

In this project we utilize the CMS open dataset and apply data analysis techniques (mainly through Python) to find the mass of the Higgs Boson. Presented at Purdue's Fall Undergraduate Research Expo with Dean Shock (Purdue Physics and Math '26), Elijah Forbes (Purdue Physics '28), and Sam Waymire (Purdue Physics '28). We are immensely grateful for Prof. Andreas Jung and Santosh Bandhari for their guidance. Download the poster pdf here.
